How we help developers

Get a glimpse on what we can offer...

Locked down dependencies Automated bots will make sure your dependencies are locked down to specific versions making your builds and deployments reproducible.

Profit from observations Thoth's aggregated knowledge helps bots perform locking to best library versions based on aggregated knowledge, not to latest which is not always greatest.

Know your runtime Get recommendations suited for hardware, operating system or other attributes defining runtime environment of your application.
